Valentino Parfumes



Valentino perfumes represent a blend of Italian luxury, romance, and modern edge, featuring 71 scents since 1978, currently produced under L'Oréal. The brand is known for iconic, rock-stud-adorned bottles, with popular lines including the romantic Donna, the sophisticated Uomo, and the highly successful Born In Roma series, featuring notes like bourbon vanilla, jasmine, and amber.

Born in Roma

Born in Roma is a popular perfume line from Valentino inspired by modern Roman spirit, elegance, confidence, and individuality. It mixes floral, woody, sweet, and fresh elements, often with a signature vanilla and jasmine presence in the women’s versions, and a spicy-woody feel in men’s versions.
